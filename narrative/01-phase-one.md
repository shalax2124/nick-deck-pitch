# Phase 1 — "The Lemon That Broke The Zen"

**Asset**: Phase 1 hero video (Super Bowl / cinematic style)
**Tone**: dramatic mockumentary, high-contrast comedy
**Tagline**: *Find the right box with LEM. Kyrious?*

> Source: [PT1 Notion](https://www.notion.so/carenbloom/PT1-HELLO-NANCY-x-NICK-KYRGIOS-350af6a34372801fa8c1c5e55e8709b1) — Crystal Lo

## Story spine
Nick built four years of zen ("haven't broken a racket in 1,460 days"). One yellow lemon-shaped object enters his life and breaks him. He hates it. The film documents his unraveling.

The audience doesn't know what the "lemon" is until the end.

## Six-scene structure

### Scene 1 — The Zen
**Location**: pristine meditation room, ethereal sunlight
**Visual**: Nick in lotus pose, ringed by old yellow tennis balls each holding a stick of incense
**Audio**: ASMR rally THWACKs blended with suggestive grunts — escalating
**Beat**: Nick's V.O. claims peace. He chants a self-control mantra. The audio crescendos.

📷 ![scene1a_zen.png](../assets/storyboard/zen.png)

### Scene 2 — The Smash (CCTV)
**Location**: locker room
**Visual**: gritty B&W security cam — Nick obliterates his racket against a metal locker
**Subtitle**: bright yellow over the silent footage — "F*** THIS LEMON!!!!"

📷 ![scene2_cctv.png](../assets/storyboard/cctv.png)

### Scene 3 — Confessional
**Location**: dark documentary interview room (Drive to Survive lighting)
**Beat**: Nick describes his girlfriend bringing home "this yellow, lemon-looking thingy." The line that sells the campaign:
> "It hits the right box every single time."

📷 ![scene3_confessional.png](../assets/storyboard/confessional.png)

### Scene 4 — The Phone Call
**Location**: Nick's living room, night
**Visual**: pacing, gesturing, intense call with unseen coach
**Beat**: tennis-serve-as-metaphor monologue — every point begins with a serve, you have to aim for the box, twice in a row is OUT. *"This yellow thing serves every single time. It never faults. It's undefeated. I hate it."*
**Action**: phone hurled across the room; primal yell

### Scene 5 — Confessional reprise
**Beat**: ego shattered, whisper-line — *"I'm supposed to be the one serving aces…"*

### Scene 6 — Title card
SMASH CUT TO BLACK.
> **Kyrious? Find the right box with LEM.**

HelloNancy logo + lemon silhouette flash. Single tennis "THWACK" SFX. Fade out.
