# Campaign Architecture

## The one-line pitch
Nick Kyrgios publicly hates a HelloNancy product, then "retaliates" by launching his own — until Phase 2 reveals the whole feud was a Trojan horse and the new product is HelloNancy's all along.

## Why it works (the strategic spine)
- **Nick is not a spokesman. He's the opponent.** Audiences don't share spokesperson ads. They share feuds.
- **The CeraVe × Michael Cera architecture**: ambiguity ("is this real?") drives organic spread. CeraVe hit 32B earned impressions in 2024 by playing it straight.
- **The reveal is the punchline**: Phase 2 turns rage into product. Nick wins, HelloNancy wins, the audience feels in on the joke.

## Two phases

| Phase | Working title | Tone | Hero asset |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** | "The Lemon That Broke The Zen" | Cinematic mockumentary, Drive-to-Survive energy | Phase 1 hero film |
| **2** | "The Kyrious Ball Launch" | Over-the-top American infomercial, revenge-arc | Phase 2 hero film |

**Tagline trajectory:**
- Phase 1: *Find the right box with LEM. Kyrious?*
- Phase 2: *Bye little lemon. You're served.*

## Tennis double-entendre engine
Tennis terms with intimacy double-meanings drive every line of copy:
- *Serve / fault / let / out / box / baseline / aim / target / rally / ace*
- Each can carry the campaign without ever being explicit.

## The narrative tension everyone will ask
> "Why the f\*\*\* is Nick Kyrgios working with a sex toy brand?"

| Wrong answer | Wrong answer | The truth |
|---|---|---|
| ~~Sponsoring them?~~ | ~~Suing them?~~ | **Trolling them.** |

Until Phase 2 — when the reveal lands that the Kyrious Ball *is* HelloNancy. The whole feud was strategy.

## Why Nick (not anyone else)
- 2022 Wimbledon finalist. 3.4M IG (@k1ngkyrg1os). Cultural figure with non-sports reach.
- Rule-breaker on record (sushi at Wimbledon, Jordans on grass, asking the crowd where to serve).
- Already candid about pressure and mental health → fits the brand's "fun, not shameful" voice.
- Funny without trying. The only athlete who can sell this without flinching.

## Production envelope (proposed)
- **One week of shoot**, June 2026
- **Primary**: Mallorca, Jun 18–21 (between Halle and Wimbledon — sun, calm, less travel pressure)
- **Hedge**: Stuttgart, Jun 5–7 (first ATP back from injury — captures content early in case of later withdrawal)
- **No clay season** in Nick's 2026 plan, so the grass run is the natural window

## Channels
1. **Organic PR seeding** — paparazzi-style "is this real?" content (CeraVe playbook)
2. **Hero branded content** — Phase 1 + Phase 2 films (Dirty Jack production credit referenced)
3. **Event integration** — pre-Wimbledon, pre-US Open tie-ins
4. **OOH amplification** — NYC, London, Melbourne walls
5. **Press stunt** — giant lemon-shaped smash cake (Nick smashes it with a racket)
6. **Merch** — Lemon Sucks tee, cap, pink wristbands, dog merch
7. **Product** — Kyrious Ball SKU (ambiguity: real product or film prop — open question)
